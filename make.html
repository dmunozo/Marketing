Locations

Our space is divided into four sections. For individuals wanting to edit their existing garments, we offer on site mending, alteration and adornment services- for changing hemlines, adding embroidered touches, replacing old zippers and patching holes.

For those wanting something custom made, we offer fabrication and tailoring services, where individuals can design a garment, or choose from a catalog of pre-fab silhouettes, and have it fabricated to their size specifications, using local deadstock, donated and upcycled textiles.

For those with less time to spend, our store is for you. In this corner we sell garments that have been designed and fabricated by our in-house design team, as well as a selection of goods from local producers.

For those wanting to learn skills, we offer a workshop. This space offers access to all sorts of tools one might need for fabricating a garment, and hosts classes on topics such as sewing, embroidery and pattern making to help facilitate the learning process. We encourage individuals to explore their own creativity, to show participants that clothing doesn't have to be a one way street.

All of these areas create a social environment that promotes learning, participation, creativity, and mindfulness. We encourage youths from neighbourhood schools to come, alone or with friends, into our shared space, to help re-envision the relationship that we have with our clothing.
